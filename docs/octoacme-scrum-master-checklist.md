# Scrum Master Responsibility Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps Scrum Masters ensure they are fulfilling their core responsibilities and facilitating effective agile practices for the team.

## Daily Responsibilities
- [ ] Facilitate daily standup (15 min timebox)
- [ ] Update team board (move items, check blockers)
- [ ] Track and follow up on blockers identified in standup
- [ ] Check with team members on impediments or concerns
- [ ] Monitor team capacity and workload balance
- [ ] Shield team from unplanned interruptions

## Sprint Planning (Beginning of Sprint)
- [ ] Prepare for sprint planning meeting (review backlog with Product Manager)
- [ ] Facilitate sprint planning session
- [ ] Ensure team has clear understanding of sprint goal
- [ ] Verify all selected items have clear acceptance criteria
- [ ] Confirm team capacity and commitment
- [ ] Check design readiness with UX Designer
- [ ] Verify infrastructure readiness with DevOps Engineer
- [ ] Document sprint goal and commitments

## During Sprint Execution
- [ ] Monitor sprint progress and burndown
- [ ] Facilitate backlog refinement sessions
- [ ] Coach team members on agile practices
- [ ] Remove or escalate impediments
- [ ] Facilitate collaboration between team members
- [ ] Protect team from scope creep
- [ ] Track metrics (velocity, cycle time, etc.)

## Sprint Review / Demo (End of Sprint)
- [ ] Schedule and facilitate sprint review meeting
- [ ] Ensure demo environment is ready
- [ ] Coordinate with team on what to demonstrate
- [ ] Invite stakeholders and ensure attendance
- [ ] Capture feedback from stakeholders
- [ ] Update product backlog based on feedback

## Sprint Retrospective
- [ ] Schedule retrospective after sprint review
- [ ] Prepare retrospective format/activity
- [ ] Facilitate retrospective discussion
- [ ] Ensure psychological safety in the discussion
- [ ] Capture action items with owners and due dates
- [ ] Follow up on previous retrospective action items
- [ ] Document key learnings

## Continuous Improvement
- [ ] Track and analyze team metrics (velocity, quality, etc.)
- [ ] Identify process improvement opportunities
- [ ] Coach team on agile principles and practices
- [ ] Foster team self-organization
- [ ] Build trust and psychological safety
- [ ] Facilitate conflict resolution when needed
- [ ] Celebrate team successes

## Stakeholder & Cross-team Collaboration
- [ ] Coordinate with Project Manager on risks and dependencies
- [ ] Collaborate with Product Manager on backlog health
- [ ] Work with UX Designer on design sprint alignment
- [ ] Partner with DevOps Engineer on deployment process improvements
- [ ] Communicate team capacity and constraints to stakeholders

## Weekly Responsibilities
- [ ] Review and update team velocity and metrics
- [ ] Check health of product backlog
- [ ] Follow up on outstanding action items
- [ ] Coordinate with other Scrum Masters (if multiple teams)
- [ ] Update team documentation and process notes

## Key Success Indicators
- Team consistently meets sprint commitments
- Blockers are resolved quickly (< 24 hours when possible)
- Team velocity is stable and predictable
- High team morale and engagement
- Effective, time-boxed ceremonies
- Continuous improvement action items are completed
- Low unplanned work and interruptions
